Generative Intelligence Lab

Research at the Frontiers of Generative AI, Robotics, and Large Models

We are a research lab at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advancing the understanding and capabilities of generative intelligence systems, bridging the gap between large-scale models and real-world applications.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Tao Hu (胡涛) leads the lab with a focus on pushing the boundaries of generative AI. He received his Ph.D. from the University of Amsterdam in 2023, where he was supervised by Prof. Cees Snoek and Prof. Pascal Mettes, followed by postdoctoral research at the renowned CompVis Group (Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich) under Prof. Björn Ommer—the team behind Stable Diffusion. His research spans diffusion models, flow matching, and efficient generative architectures, with publications at top-tier venues including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, and T-PAMI. Notable contributions include Self-Guided Diffusion Models (CVPR 2023), which pioneered self-supervised guidance for diffusion; ZigMa (ECCV 2024), the first DiT-style Mamba-based diffusion model; DepthFM (AAAI 2025 Oral), achieving fast monocular depth estimation via flow matching; and ToddlerDiffusion (ICLR 2025), enabling interpretable controllable generation. He has received multiple oral and spotlight presentations, serves as Area Chair for CVPR workshops on AI for Content Creation and Efficient Large Vision Models, and was recognized as an Excellent Reviewer at NeurIPS 2024.

Research Areas

  • Generative AI - Diffusion models, flow matching, image/video synthesis, controllable generation
  • Large Vision Models - Foundation models, multimodal learning, efficient training and inference
  • Embodied AI - Robot learning, sim-to-real transfer, physical intelligence
  • Brain-Computer Interface (脑机接口) - Neural signal processing, human-machine interaction

PhD Students (博士生)

We are looking for PhD candidates with:

  • Strong background in computer science, mathematics, or related fields
  • Programming skills in Python and deep learning frameworks (PyTorch)
  • Interest in generative models, computer vision, or machine learning
  • Self-motivation and passion for research

Master Students (硕士生)

Ideal candidates should have:

  • Solid foundation in machine learning and deep learning
  • Good programming and mathematical skills
  • Enthusiasm for cutting-edge AI research

Undergraduate Research Assistants (本科生科研助理)

We welcome talented undergraduates who want to:

  • Gain hands-on research experience in AI
  • Work on real projects with potential for publication
  • Prepare for graduate studies
